## Overview
Congratulations on getting your hands on a FANTM DEVLPR! We think they are a load of fun, and we're constantly working to make them easier to pick up and play with.
All you need to do is put a jumper on the male header connecting the EMG signal to whichever analog pin you want to use. This also lets you stack multiple EMG sensors and read from lots of muscles at once.
